  - Referencing (normalized)

- What are the trade-offs of embedding? (document size limit, update amplification)
- What are the trade-offs of referencing? (multiple queries, no join support)
- What are the three types of relationships in data modeling?
  - One-to-One, One-to-Many, Many-to-Many
- How do you model a One-to-One relationship? (embedding preferred)
- How do you model a One-to-Many relationship? (embedding for bounded, referencing for unbounded)
- How do you model a Many-to-Many relationship in MongoDB? (arrays of references)
- What is the 16 MB document size limit, and how does it affect schema design?
- What is an unbounded array? Why is it dangerous in MongoDB?

- What is the **Computed Pattern**? When do you use it?

- What is the **Extended Reference Pattern**? What problem does it solve?
- What is the **Schema Versioning Pattern**? How do you migrate schema incrementally?
- What is the **Tree Pattern**? What are the variants?
  - Parent References, Child References, Materialized Paths, Nested Sets
- What is the **Polymorphic Pattern**? When is it useful?
- What is the **Approximation Pattern**?
- What is the **Pre-Allocation Pattern**?
- What is **denormalization**? Why does MongoDB encourage it (with trade-offs)?
- What is **data duplication** in MongoDB? When is it acceptable?
- How do you handle schema evolution in a production MongoDB collection?
- What is `$jsonSchema` validator? How do you enforce a schema in MongoDB?
- How do you add validation rules to an existing collection?
- What is `validationAction: "error"` vs `"warn"`?
- What is `validationLevel: "strict"` vs `"moderate"`?
